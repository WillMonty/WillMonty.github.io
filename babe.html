  • Resumé

Bar ain't big enough is a 4-player fighting game themed around a Western Bar Brawl. The game was made in Unity by a team of five.

I implemented the throwing system, drinking mechanic, and xbox controller functionality. I also created all of the environment and item art as well as all of the sound effects in the game.
