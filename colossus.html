About the Game:
Project Colossus is a local multiplayer VR game made with Unity and SteamVR for the Vive. The current experience was created with a student team in 15 weeks. I stood as lead VR developer, implementing the robot "colossus" character and its abilities. I also created all of the sound effects from scratch.
Using the Vive, one player becomes the giant "Colossus" and attempts to eliminate the other two players before they are destroyed. Using controllers, the other two players play as "Resistance" soldiers trying to defeat the Colossus without being eliminated.
Currently, we are working towards our second Imagine RIT presentation to show off our new classes and abilities.
